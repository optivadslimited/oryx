01 — Executive Summary
Oryx Eyewear is a premium eyewear brand founded in Morocco, positioned between Western luxury confidence and Moroccan understated elegance. The brand targets style-aware, entrepreneurial 20–35 year-olds in Morocco's major cities and global markets.
The brand DNA is anchored in one truth: "I know who I am — I don't need validation." Oryx does not sell glasses. It sells clarity, focus, and quiet confidence.
The launch strategy centers on two signature frames — each with a name, two colorways (Black, Tortoise, Brown, Grey) — and a dual-channel go-to-market: paid social media advertising and strategic influencer collaborations.

06 — Go-To-Market Strategy
Phase 1: Brand Foundation (Pre-Launch, Months 1–2)
Before selling anything, build desire. The brand must exist in culture before it exists in commerce.
Build Instagram and TikTok presence with intentional, minimal content
Develop brand identity assets: photography, video, copy tone
Seed product with 5–10 carefully chosen early collaborators (non-paid)
Build e-commerce store (Shopify or equivalent)
Create waitlist or early access mechanism

Phase 2: Launch (Month 3)
Drop Frame 01 with a defined launch moment (date, narrative, visual campaign)
Activate paid social: Instagram and TikTok ads
Launch influencer collaboration wave (see Influencer Strategy)
Frame 02 drops 3–4 weeks after Frame 01 to sustain momentum

Phase 3: Scale (Months 4–12)
Optimize paid ads based on performance data from launch
Expand influencer tier to mega creators and international profiles
Explore pop-up retail in Casablanca or Marrakech
Begin international shipping and marketing
Evaluate a 3rd frame or new colorways based on sell-through data

07 — Digital Strategy & Content
Platform Priority
Instagram — primary brand-building platform. Aesthetic-first. Longer shelf life.
TikTok — reach and discovery engine. Storytelling, founder content, product drops.
Website / E-commerce — conversion point. Must reflect the same premium feel as the brand.

Content Pillars
The Object — product photography, flat lay, detail shots. Clean, minimal, intentional.
The Person — lifestyle content. Who wears Oryx. How they live, move, and work.
The Story — brand narrative. Origin, founder, Morocco, process.
The Drop — launch content, countdown, unboxing, collab reveals.

Paid Advertising
Paid ads are the primary customer acquisition channel at launch. The strategy prioritizes quality over volume — build desire before driving traffic.
Meta Ads (Instagram + Facebook): retargeting, lookalike audiences, interest targeting (fashion, entrepreneurship, luxury lifestyle)
TikTok Ads: spark ads boosting organic content from collaborators
Target: Morocco first, expand to FR/ES/NL diaspora in Phase 2
Budget allocation: 60% Meta, 40% TikTok at launch

Key Metrics to Track
Cost per purchase (CPP) — keep below 30€ in Morocco
Return on Ad Spend (ROAS) — target 3x minimum
Instagram follower quality — engagement rate over follower count
Email list growth — build owned audience from day one

09 — Operations & Supply Chain
Manufacturing
Source manufacturers in China (Wenzhou / Shenzhen) or Italy for premium positioning
Minimum Order Quantities (MOQ) typically 100–300 units per SKU at launch
Request samples before full production — quality control is non-negotiable
Target COGS of 20–30€ per unit inclusive of frame, lenses, and case

Inventory Strategy
Launch with limited stock — scarcity drives desire
Start with 150–300 units total across 4 SKUs
Reorder based on sell-through data — do not over-invest in inventory
Keep 2–4 week reorder buffer to avoid stockouts during peak periods

Fulfillment
Morocco: self-fulfill or partner with local courier (Amana, Aramex)
International: integrate with shipping aggregator for competitive rates
Packaging must reflect the brand — unboxing is a brand touchpoint
Aim for 24–48 hour Morocco dispatch, 5–10 day international delivery

E-Commerce Platform
Shopify recommended — proven for DTC brands, strong analytics
Domain: oryxeyewear.com
Integrate: Klaviyo (email), Meta Pixel, TikTok Pixel, Google Analytics
Photography and copy must match brand voice — no generic product descriptions

12 — Long-Term Vision
Year 1: Prove the Model
Sell 500–1,000 units. Build a brand with cultural credibility. Create content people share without being asked. Prove that a Moroccan premium brand can command global prices.
Year 2: Expand the World
Launch 2 more frames. Open a physical presence — a concept store or pop-up in Casablanca or Marrakech. Begin wholesale conversations with select premium boutiques in Paris, Madrid, and Amsterdam. Grow the team.
Year 3: Global Brand
Oryx is known. Not just in Morocco — in the cafes of Paris, on the beaches of Monaco, in the boardrooms of Dubai. The brand has earned its place alongside the names that inspired it. The origin story is the competitive moat. Born in Morocco. Built for everywhere.
